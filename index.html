Argentinian Italian-Style Milanesa Napolitana

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: 4 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Thinly sliced 4
Eggs 3
Oregano 1 teaspoon
Sañt and pepper to taste
Bread crumbs 2 cups
Parmesan cheese 1/4
Garlic 2 cloves
Olive oil 1/3 cup
Tomato sauce 1/2 cup
Deli ham 4 slices
Mozzarella cheese 1 cup

Step 1: Gather the ingredients.

Step 2: Place the eggs in a shallow bowl or pan, and whisk them together with the oregano and some salt and pepper.

Step 3: Stir the parmesan cheese and garlic into the bread crumbs and place them in another shallow pan.

Step 4: Dip the steaks first in the egg mixture, then in the bread crumbs, coating them well with the crumbs.

Step 5: Heat the olive oil in a heavy skillet, and cook steaks for several minutes on each side, until golden brown and crispy. Drain steaks on paper towels.

Step 6: Place steaks on a baking sheet. Turn on oven broiler.

Step 7: Top each steak with a slice of ham, 2 to 3 tablespoons tomato sauce, and 1/4 cup grated mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle Italian seasoning over the cheese and place steaks under ​the broiler until cheese melts.

Step 8: Serve warm, with fries.

Step 9: Enjoy!

Aji de Gallina: Peruvian Spicy Creamed Chicken

  • Time: 60 mins
  • Serves: 6 portions
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 4
White bread 4
Evaporated milk 3/4 cup
Chicken breast 1 1/2 pounds
Chicken stock 4 cups
Aji peppers 3 to 4
Garlic 2 cloves
Olive oil 1/3 cup
Onion 1 large
Black olives 10
Hard-boiled eggs 2

Step 1: Gather the ingredients.

Step 2: Cook the yellow potatoes in salted water until tender when pierced with a fork.

Step 3:Let cool, peel, cut into quarters, and set aside.

Step 4: Place the bread in a small bowl and pour the evaporated milk over it to soak. Set aside.

Step 5: Place the chicken breasts in a pot with the chicken stock and bring to a simmer.

Step 6: Cook for 10 to 15 minutes until chicken is just barely cooked through.

Step 7:Set chicken aside to cool.

Step 8: Strain broth and reserve 2 cups

Step 9:Remove stems and seeds from the peppers.

Step 9:In a blender, process peppers with the vegetable oil until smooth.

Step 9:Sauté the garlic and onions with the puréed peppers and oil until the onions are soft and golden.

Step 9:Remove from heat and let cool.

Step 9:Shred the cooled chicken into bite-size pieces.

Step 9:In a blender or food processor, process the evaporated milk and bread mixture with the nuts and Parmesan cheese until smooth.

Step 9:Add the cooked onion mixture and process briefly.

Step 9:Return onion mixture to the pan, and add 1 1/2 cups of the reserved chicken stock.

Step 9:Bring to a low simmer, and stir in the chicken. Heat until warmed through, adding more chicken stock if the sauce is too thick.

Step 9:Serve over rice, garnished with the yellow potatoes, slices of hard-boiled egg and black olives.

Step 9:Enjoy!
